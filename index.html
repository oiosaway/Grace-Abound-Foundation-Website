About Us

The Grace Abound For Women Foundation is all about women lifting up other women by sharing, caring, empowering and so much more. We are women reaching out to women, especially those in distress, abusive relationships, divorced women, widows and indeed all women. We aim to create an environment where women can flourish and be fruitful. We desire that women become who God has called them to be.
